Manage Categories

Functionality: Create/Delete/Edit Categories displayed in kiosk Menu. Change categories order (sequence) shown in menu list. Temporary hide category from be displayed. Assign Tenants and People to category Quick preview who is in category listing always viewable. List “Not in Category” can be displayed at the same time with all other records not included in current category for better visualization. Listing easy to manage and view result on screen right away. Both lists support multi-select operation.
